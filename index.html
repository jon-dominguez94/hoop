    How to Play

  • Use your mouse to draw a full circle around blue orbs
  • Drawn circles must be fully enclosed! Valid circles are filled in with a purple shade
  • Avoid red bomb orbs!

    Information

  • All orbs have a specific lifetime; enclose blue orbs before they die
  • Blue orbs will flash bright pink when they are almost dead
  • Health is awarded for enclosing blue orbs
  • Health is lost when blue orbs die or when enclosing red, bomb orbs
  • Play until health is depleted
  • Score is awarded for enclosing blue orbs and for total time played

    Multiplier

  • The multiplier increases the rate at which you earn points
  • Increase the multiplier by going on streaks
  • Enclosing red orbs or letting blue ones die resets your multiplier
